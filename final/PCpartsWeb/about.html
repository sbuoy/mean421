About Us It's Nice to Meet You!

The Mean PC Parts team has gathered together to built a working site. Using our lessons from school we've built a site that fulfills all that the customers and employers will need while still retaining its flexibility to grow larger if need be. It started with building a site and then having it store data valuables and accounts, with those done all we needed was to build the site around them. Soon we expanded into building the other pages. Along the way, we had to solve issues with minor things such as the lost password function and getting the proper feedback from the various tools. We persevered and built a site we can be proud of.

With this site, we made it so one can order various products like pc parts. The site is also the best it can get in terms of like-ability as it is fast and streamlined in terms of use and check out. After all, nobody wants to be stuck searching a site for what you want.
