General questions

How do I sign up?

  • When you land on the page click on the Register dropdown tab then select “Create One.”

  • Then you should be directed to the registration page.

  • Next you will fill out the fields which are all required.

  • First you will enter a first and last name. If you accidentally enter in a number or special character in your name or simply make a typo, do not worry you will be able to update it when you verify your account and log into it.

  • Next enter in the country you live in, abbreviations like USA, CAN or MEX are acceptable and can be changed later.

  • Next enter in your address, make sure that it a valid address to help facilitate the shipping and packaging process for the supplier. You can use https://smartystreets.com/products/single-address to verify address.

  • Next enter in your city, state and zipcode. When you enter in your zipcode make sure that is exactly 5 digits long. To verify you can use https://smartystreets.com/products/single-address If you don’t live in the United States in the states field you can type in N/A and in zipcode 00000.

  • For phone number be sure to enter it in this 10 digit format, for example 2156772829.

  • Next enter in your email address, it must include a @ to designate that it is an email address. If you are not sure your email is verified you can use https://verify-email.org/

How to setup password?

  • The password length must be greater than or equal to six characters.

  • A password, for example fives, would return a password exception alert box saying that it needs to be at least 6 characters or more.

  • It is highly recommended that your password has a mixture of special characters or numbers in addition to letters.

  • A password like monkey or password, can be easily stolen by hackers or others guessing passwords.

  • You want to use a password like MeanPCparts*18, to ensure your account security.

  • Also, it would be a good idea to save your password into your browser if you choose to do so, to prevent yourself from having to reset your password.

How do I verify my account?

  • You should be redirected to the verification page once the “Register” button is clicked and all the fields are appropriately filled out.

  • You will then re-enter your email and the verification code received by the email.

  • A verification successful pop up should appear if correct.

  • If you enter in the wrong code or the code is not exactly 6 digits it will return a pop message saying combination not found and will have to refresh the page and enter in the information again.

How do I login?

  • To login navigate back to the Register tab on the homepage enter in your email and password and login.

How do I get to my account page?

  • You are redirected to your account page always after logging in.

  • You can also access your account page by clicking account details in the Register box which is now showing your name.

How do I update account information?

  • This is where you can update your account information fields.

  • For example, you want to change USA to United States. You can simply type United States in the Country field and click the update button and it will update your information.

What do I do if I forgot my password?

  • If you forgot your password you can reset it by navigating to the Register dropdown tab, click on “Forgot Password.”

  • A popup box will appear, enter the email address to the account in which you forgot your password.

  • You will follow the same process as to when you originally verified your account.

  • Click on change password, and it should say Successfully changed password.

  • You can now login with your new password.

How do I logout from my account?

  • To sign out of your account go to the top right hand corner box where your name is displayed and click sign out.

  • The place where your name was displayed should change back to Register.

Purchasing

I want to buy my items - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Select your desired items
  • Add them to your cart
  • Checkout

Adding our products to your cart

  • While on the shop page you can choose a MEAN PCparts product of choice and by clicking the Add To Cart button you can add it to the cart.

  • You will know the product is added to the cart because the cart now has 1 object inside it.

Shopping Cart

  • To post a product to your cart, you went on the Shop page and added in an item.

  • If you want to add another item to your cart go back to the shop page and add another item to the cart, your total will be updated accordingly in the Sales Summary box to the right of the table.

  • Navigate to checkout page by clicking checkout.

How do I clear the cart?

  • The clear out your cart, click clear cart under where it says checkout.

  • To remove an individual product, click on remove next to the product or custom you want to remove.

How do I checkout?

  • On the checkout page you will see the sales summary of your payment at the top of the page.

  • Next you will scroll down and fill out the shipping address information.

  • Filling out the shipping address is similar to when you registered your account.

  • Make sure the information you enter is accurate, especially the email and address.

What are the payment options?

  • To finish up checking out you will enter in card information, it can be credit, debit, pre-paid or a gift card as long as it is valid.

  • You can use https://www.bincodes.com/creditcard-checker/ to check if the card you are using is a valid number.

  • This website encrypts credit card information and uses https to make sure your credit information is stolen by a third party. We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and PayPal).

How do I get a receipt and view my order contents?

  • When you successfully checkout you should be redirected to a confirmation page and then receive an email confirmation of your purchase.

  • Your email you entered in when filling out the shipping address fields should have a copy of your sales order receipt.

  • If you do not see the receipt in your inbox. Check your spam inbox.

What are the payment options?

  • Navigate back to your account page and click on “Order”, you will then be redirected to a page that shows your orders.

I want to buy PC Parts - what are the steps?

Buying on MEAN PCparts is really simple. Once you have added your items to the cart, which is to your liking, you can quickly and securely pay via Visa, American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and PayPal. Once the transaction is complete, you will receive your items with 3 to 5 business days.
